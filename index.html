SERVICES WE PROVIDE

By the end of our program, your child will be an advanced programmer, capable of devising industry-standard algorithms and ready for real internship work. Your child will have a side-project completed, and a refined resume to jumpstart their career.

Resume Building

During one session, the advisors will help students craft their resumes from scratch using a professional template. We will teach them what to and what not to include, and set the stage for future resume edits in later years.

Programming

The advisors will briefly review previous material and then cover 2-3 new key concepts every session. Just as in Berkeley, the course will be taught in Python, but the concepts will be taught as to be applied to any language.
